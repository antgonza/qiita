Welcome to Qiita

Qiita is a development effort powering the data analysis and storage for phase two of the Human Microbiome Project, a new three-year "multi-omic" study investigating the roles played by microbes and their interactions with the human body. This project as a model will delve into the relationship between the microbiome and disease, using inflammatory bowel disease as a model.
